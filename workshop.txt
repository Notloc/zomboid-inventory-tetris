description=[h1]Inventory Tetris[/h1]
description=Inventory Tetris is a comprehensive inventory overhaul for Project Zomboid.
description=[list]
description=[*] The inventory is replaced with a grid based system.
description=[*] All items now have a size.
description=[*] [b]See inside all your equipped bags at once[/b]
description=[*] Bags now provide additional inventory space, not just weight reduction.
description=[*] Worn items do not take up inventory space.
description=[*] No more shoving 3 boxes of cereal and a carton of milk in your pants.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]CONTROLS[/h1]
description=[h2]Keyboard & Mouse[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b][i]Drag and Drop[/i][/b] - Almost everything can be dragged and dropped across the inventory
description=[*] [b][I]Split Stacks[/i][/b] - Drag and drop while holding [b]CTRL[/b]
description=[*] [b][i]Popup Window![/i][/b] - Double click on containers to open them in a popup window
description=[*] [b][i]Quick Gather[/i][/b] - Double click on stackable items to quickly gather them up
description=[*] [b][i]Rotate items[/i][/b] - Press [b]R[/b]*
description=[*] [b][i]Quick Move[/i][/b] - Hold [b]CTRL[/b] + Left Click
description=[*] [b][i]Quick Equip[/i][/b] - Hold [b]ALT[/b] + Left Click
description=[/list]
description=[b][i]Check out keybinds and mod options to customize these controls![/i][/b]
description=
description=[h2]Controller[/h2]
description=[b][i]Written in Xbox[/i][/b]
description=
description=[b]General[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] A - Menu
description=[*] B - Pickup
description=[*] X - Quick move
description=[*] Y - Close inventory/popup
description=[*] SELECT - Open equipment panel
description=[*] LB - Change/select inventory window
description=[*] RB - Change/select loot window
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]While holding item[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] A - Rotate
description=[*] B - Drop
description=[*] Y - Cancel
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]While hovering over a container[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*] B+B - Open container popup
description=[/list]

description=[h1]TRAITS[/h1]
description=Inventory Tetris makes sure to give new uses to the vanilla traits related to the inventory!
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Organized[/b] - Does nothing right now, will improve autosort when it is added.
description=[*] [b]Disorganized[/b] - When using quick move, items will not always stay stacked and will end up in a random spot in the grid. Cannot autosort (in the future when its added).
description=
description=[*] [b]Dextrous[/b] - Faster container searching.
description=[*] [b]All Thumbs[/b] - Slower container searching.
description=[/list]

description=[h1]Search Mode![/h1]
description=[i]Use sandbox settings when creating your world to enabled searching!
description=When enabled, containers must be searched before they can be looted.[/i]

description=[h1]High FPS Tetris[/h1]
description=By default, Zomboid's UI is only rendered at 20fps, due to performance concerns.
description=If you have a powerful computer, you may increase the UI's FPS to match the game's framerate in the game options:
description=[code]Options -> Display -> UI -> UI Offscreen Rendering -> UNCHECK[/code]
description=This will have a performance cost but the UI, particularly the inventory, will be more responsive.
description=
description=[h1]FAQ[/h1]
description=[h2]How do I open the keyring?[/h2]
description=Double click it. The items will be shown in a pop-up window.
description=
description=[h2]Furniture is too big for my bag![/h2]
description=Use your hands. Equip the furniture as you would a weapon.
description=Your hands can only hold 1 item each, but the items they hold can be as big as you want.
description=
description=[h2]My game lags / Low FPS[/h2]
description=Ensure that the following game options is CHECKED:
description=[code]Options -> Display -> UI -> UI Offscreen Rendering -> CHECK[/code]
description=
description=[h2]Can I add/remove this on existing saves?[/h2]
description=Yes.
description=Inventory Tetris can added/removed at any point without issue.
description=(Note: When adding the mod the player may drop items to the ground if they do not have enough space.)
